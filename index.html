About

What is Blood Donation

Nowadays people donate blood to save human lives. There are some persons who need to change the total blood of their bodies at regular interval. Blood is also necessary to be transfused in the body of the patients who undergo serious operations. Patients also need blood to make good the loss on account of accidents.

Why to Donate Blood

Blood is the most precious gift that anyone can give to another person — the gift of life. A decision to donate your blood can save a life, or even several if your blood is separated into its components — red cells, platelets and plasma — which can be used individually for patients with specific conditions.
